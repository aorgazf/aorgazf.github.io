About

Álvaro Orgaz is an Information Security professional currently working for a National Security company in Abu Dhabi. At the age of 9 started coding on a Commodore PET 4032 until it ran out of memory, since then he has been passionate about computers, electronics and technology.

He enjoys researching and engaging into all sorts of hacking projects to keep his skills sharp, from hardware security to machine learning or reverse engineering. He writes as a self-development exercise that helps him gain a deeper understanding of the topics he researches and build a personal knowledge repository.

He would like to contribute to keeping organisations and people safe from the increasing professionalisation of cybercrime. With his posts he wishes to bring awareness of the multiple threats and provide advice on how to defend from them.

Álvaro is always interested in collaboration opportunities and new challenges. Connect with him on LinkedIn or Github

Education

Álvaro has a MSc. Engineering degree, over 18 years’ experience managing complex, high-value projects and holds valid CISSP and PMP certifications.
